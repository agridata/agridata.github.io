Welcome to AgriData, a data visualization website for agriculture lovers, garden fanatics, and anyone who is simply interested in the topographical layout of Florida! Our team of computer scientists and developers have built this map to display the most optimal areas for cultivating agriculture in the state of Florida. We hope you will find this tool useful and share it with your like-minded friends and family!
