About the Developers

AgriData's team consists of four young women. Through the Girls Who Code Summer Immersion Program (2017), we built AgriData as a collaborative project to share with all of you. Each member of our team contributes to the website in her own unique way. You can read about them individually below.

Daniella is the head of our backend development. In the fall, she will be a senior at School for Advanced Studies North. She enjoys math. Her favorite language is Python. She plans to pursue Computer Science and Engineering.
